Wave and Ground Surfers.
Inspired by Art, Travel and Surf. Support local Artists.

Wags is an independent surf brand that combines Surf with Art, producing clothing
using traditional printing techniques, making each piece unique and coming in limited editions.
We explore the relationship "Art and Surf" supporting Artists, Designers, Illustrators,
Photographers and Creatives that are from the Country. We support the locals!
Wags is used as a plataform where brand and artist help each other.
